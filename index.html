TactiGrid

    Goal:

  • The first player to reach the opposite corner wins.(Opponent's starting tile).
  • The other way to win is with a successful attack.

    • Movement and Action Buttons:

  • Arrow Btns = move. To remain still, press "Camouflage"
  • "Camouflage" also makes the player the same color as the tile that the player is on.
  • "Glow an opponent" removes the camouflage, and opponent becomes visible.
  • "Attack" has a range of two Manhattan steps. If attack succeeds, you win! If attack fails, the opponent wins!
  • The colored 'doors' at the edges will funnel you back to the center of the grid.
